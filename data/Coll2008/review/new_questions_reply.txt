Further questions about the study: Coll2008

1. Could you please indicate the functional type of each species? Please answer using one of the following categories: EA = evergreen angiosperm; DA = deciduous angiosperm; EG = evergreen gymnosperm; DG = deciduous gymnosperm

All the species are decidous angiosperms (DA).

- Cedrela odorata: DA
- Tabebuia rosea: DA
- Enterolobium cyclocarpum: DA
- Hura crepitans: DA 
- Sterculia apetala: DA
- Antirrhoea trichantha: DA
- Luehea seemannii: DA
- Cordia alliodora: DA
